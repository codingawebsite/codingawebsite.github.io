Chapter 5: Images

Images.

You can use images to diplay visual content in a webpage to enhance user experience and convey information.

Example HTML Code:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<body>
    <p>This is a Normal Image.</p>

    <img src="dinosaur.png" alt="A picture of a dinosaur"/>
  
    <p>Make image clickable</p>
<href="https://en.wikipedia.org">

<img src="dinosaur.png" alt="A picture of a dinosaur"/>
</a>

</body>
</html>

Output

This is a Normal Image

        

        
Make image clickable
