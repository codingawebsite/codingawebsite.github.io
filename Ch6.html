Tables in HTML

You can use tabels to organize data into rows and columns, using specific tags.

Example HTML Code:

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<body>
 <table>
    <p>Monthly Sales Data</p>

    <thead>
    <tr>
  
   <th>Month</th>
   <th>Sales</th>
   </tr>
   </thead>
   
   <tbody>

   <tr>
   <td>January</td>
   <td>$5,000</td>
   </tr>

   <tr>
   <td>February</td>
   <td>$3,500</td>
   </tr>


</body>
</html>

Output

 

    Monthly Sales Data
  

  
  
    
    

      Month
      Sales
    

  
  
    
    

      January
      $5,000
    

    

      February
      $3,500
